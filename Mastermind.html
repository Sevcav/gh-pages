Scoring: You get one small black peg for a guess of the right color in the right location and one small white peg for a guess of of the right color but in the wrong position.
The relative locations of the scoring (small black and white) pegs does not necessarily reveal which pegs in your guess are correct.

    How to Play:
  1. Select a colored peg from the tray by clicking the desired color. The color selected will be dispalyed in the upper right of board.
  2. Starting at Turn 1, place a colored peg in each of the holes. Just click on the hole to place a peg of the Active Color.
  3. You can change the color of any peg in the current row by selecting a new Active Color, and clicking on the peg you want to change.
  4. Click the scoring flag for that turn (Square with 4 holes) to see how closely your guess matches the secret vault pattern.
  5. Proceed to the next row using the information provided by the scoring of previous rows.
  6. When your guess matches the secret pattern, you win!
  7. After 8 turns you still have not matched the secret pattern, the game will end and show the secret vault pattern.
